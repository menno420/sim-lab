where every cyclic edge sits at exactly 2/3 is optimal — no four-dice set beats
2/3 on all cyclic edges (the four-dice ceiling). A cheap orthogonal extension:

maximum uniform cyclic win-probability achievable at n=4,5,6 dice, locating where
the 2/3 ceiling gives way (Steinhaus–Trybula bound) — it turns "these four
