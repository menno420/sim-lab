# VERDICT 159 — quota-attainment cliff bunching: a 100%-quota comp accelerator re-times deals across the period boundary, so observed bookings show bunching + spurious negative lag-1 autocorrelation while horizon demand is exactly conserved

Reproduce PROPOSAL 146 (round-34 VENTURE slot, P146 → V159, +13): the quota-attainment cliff bunching artifact. A sales-comp ACCELERATOR with a CLIFF at 100% quota — marginal deals below quota pay the base rate, above quota the accelerated rate — is a convex kink in each rep's payoff, so rational reps RE-TIME deals across the period boundary. Two rational relocations follow: (i) a rep just under quota (attainment in [1−PULL_WINDOW, 1)) pulls next-period deals FORWARD, smallest-first, just enough to CLEAR the cliff → lands just above quota (BUNCHING in the [Q, Q(1+eps)] band); (ii) a rep comfortably over quota (attainment ≥ 1+OVER_THRESH, the accelerator zone) pulls forward MORE next-period deals, largest-first up to ACCEL_BANK_CAP quota-units, to book at the accelerated rate NOW ("a hot period borrows from the next" → depletes the successor period). The folk belief: "a comp cliff at 100% quota just sharpens focus on hitting quota; it changes how reps are PAID, not what/when they sell — aggregate bookings and their trend are unaffected, and most reps landing right at quota means the plan is well-calibrated." It is NOT: aggregated over N_REPS × N_PERIODS with TRUE demand i.i.d. across periods, the OBSERVED (relocated) series shows three artifacts absent from the TRUE counterfactual — (1) BUNCHING (excess band mass), (2) a SPURIOUS NEGATIVE lag-1 autocorrelation of team totals (a hot period is followed by a depleted one), (3) EXACT horizon CONSERVATION (relocation moves deals only between existing adjacent periods, no leak past the last period) — so the volatility / mean-reversion a forecaster reads off QoQ booking growth is a comp-plan ARTIFACT, not the market. The estimator is a PAIRED Monte-Carlo difference on the /se convention (z = mean_diff / (stdev_diff/sqrt(M_TRIALS))): per trial re-draw true demand, derive the observed grid by the deterministic relocation rule, compute (observed − counterfactual) per statistic, netting out the shared finite-sample autocorrelation bias. Three ordered gates (G1→G2→G3, APPROVE iff ALL): G1 BUNCHING band excess z ≥ 3 (one-sided); G2 SPURIOUS NEGATIVE lag-1 autocorr — diff < 0 and magnitude z ≥ 3; G3 CONSERVATION (≤ 1e-6 both worlds) + ROBUSTNESS (Dist B still ≥ 3σ). The trap: a forecaster who reads mean-reversion off observed QoQ bookings is measuring the comp plan, not demand. LOAD-BEARING disclosed model choice (the P024 discipline): a pure above-quota accelerator with NO quota ratchet — under a RATCHET plan an over-quota rep faces the opposite marginal incentive (defer/sandbag), which flips the autocorrelation sign toward smoothing; bunching and conservation are ratchet-robust, the G2 SIGN is regime-dependent.
