💡 Session idea: the rollover convention's "originally appended @ <short-SHA>"
stub field was recoverable only by replaying `git log --reverse -p` over the
whole outbox history — an O(file-history) cost every roll pays. If the kit's
outbox grammar stamped each block with its landing SHA at append time (one
extra field, written by the session that appends), every future roll becomes
a pure text transform and the stub SHAs become self-evidently trustworthy
rather than reconstructed. Worth an ASK to substrate-kit alongside its
pending advisory size check.
