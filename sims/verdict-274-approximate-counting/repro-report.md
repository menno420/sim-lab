## Claim reproduced

A counter `C` starts at `C_0 = 0`; to process one increment, draw `u ~ Uniform(0,1)` and set `C := C+1` iff `u < 2^(−C)`, else leave `C` unchanged; after `n` increments the estimator is `N̂ = 2^{C_n} − 1`.
